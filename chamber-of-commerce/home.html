Purpose

The Aurora Chamber of Commerce is the voice of the business community in the Eastern Metro area. It is an action-oriented organization, serving as a powerful advocate for its members.

A primary objective of The Chamber is to create a positive business environment and encourage growth and long-term economic vitality. Chamber members can access key business, political, and community leaders, in order to influence the course of commerce on all levels.

The Chamber exists to meet the needs of the business community.
