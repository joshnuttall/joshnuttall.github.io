Aurora

Aurora is the 3rd most populous city in the state of Colorado. Aurora originated in the 1880s and was originally called the town of Fletcher. In 1907 the town of Fletcher name was changed to Aurora named after one of the subdivisions.In the 1970s and early 1980s Aurora was one of the fastest growing citites in America.Aurora has had a large militray prescence since the early 1900s when Army General Hospital #21 was opened. It also has two Air Force Bases with one currently being used.
