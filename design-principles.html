Design Principles

White Space

Beehive Federal Credit Union

Beehive.Org

Beehive's website's use of white space allows everything to look nice and spaced out. If there wasn't whitespace all the information would be very cramped together and would be very difficult to read.

Repetition

ESPN

ESPN.com

ESPN's website's use of repititon can be see in with how they style their font. They consistently color the font red for the current time in the game. Their consistent use of fonts for different things make it much easier for the user to understand their website and to use it.

Contrast

Amazon

Amazon.com

Amazon's website's use of Contrast can be seen with the dark background and use of white color of font and of the ribbion like things. Specifically the background that has the Prime logo with the black background of the overall website makes the Prime logo stick out. So, not only does contrast allow the user to easily look at your website, but it can be used to point things out.
