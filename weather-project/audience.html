The target audience of this site are those who rely on weather forecasts for things like travel, work, outdoor events, etc.

Brad

Brad is a CEO for a company and does a lot of traveling by car/plane. He uses weather forecast to plan when he will make long trips and what times specifically to book flights so that he can be as efficent as possible wit his business trips.

Lisa

Lisa is a young mom of 2 kids. One of her current hobbies is running. Lisa prefers to run outside when she trains for her marathons. If shes knows it will be rainy or snowy, she can adjust her schedule so that she is able to run in the local gym.
